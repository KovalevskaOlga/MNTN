Discover Your Hiking Level

Take this quick and fun quiz to find out what kind of hiker you really are — whether you’re a beginner just starting out, a seasoned trail explorer, or an expert backpacker ready for the toughest adventures. Knowing your hiking level helps you choose the right trails, prepare properly, and enjoy every step of your journey safely and confidently. Ready to unlock your hiking potential? Let’s get started!

Hiking Skill Levels

  • Novice Hiker

    Just starting out — familiar with basic hiking gear and simple trails. Learning to prepare and stay safe on short hikes.

  • Moderate Hiker

    Comfortable with moderate trails, can plan day hikes and handle changing weather conditions confidently.

  • Advanced Moderate Hiker

    Experienced on longer and more challenging hikes, skilled in navigation and basic survival techniques.

  • Expert Hiker

    Proficient in difficult terrains, equipped for multi-day hikes, knowledgeable about first aid and mountain safety.

  • Expert Backpacker

    Highly skilled and experienced, capable of leading expeditions and managing extreme conditions in the wilderness.
